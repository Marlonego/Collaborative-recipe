# Salad Recipe

- Ingredient

- Tomato

- Onions

- Garlic

- Lettuce

- Cucumber

-  Olive Oil

- Salt

- Pepper

- Lemon

- Procedure:

- Chop all the vegetables.

- Put them in a big bowl.

- Add olive oil, salt, pepper and lemon.

- Take a big spoon and mix everything well.

- Taste to see if you want to add anything.

- Enjoy!
